About

I am a seasoned product design researcher with a doctoral degree in computer engineering and over 7 years of industry experience. While I started my career as a lecturer, since completing my doctoral degree in 2013, I am working as an applied researcher, first in USA and now in UAE, working on projects involving global customers across various industries like Aviation, Healthcare, Power & Water, Energy, and Transportation. The industry experience has provided me the opportunity to apply my research work to real world problems and improve operational products across various industries. Over the years, I have been responsible for identifying, designing, and developing innovative user interfaces and visualizations both for the analysis of underlying domain specific datasets and for the effective dissemination of information to users.
